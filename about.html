Our Story

Crafting the perfect pizza since 2010

The Tiko's Pizza Journey

Founded in 2010, Tiko's Pizza began as a small family-owned pizzeria in Nairobi. Our passion for creating the perfect pizza, combined with our commitment to using only the freshest ingredients, quickly earned us a reputation as one of Kenya's premier pizza destinations.

Today, we continue to honor our traditional recipes while innovating to meet modern tastes. Every pizza we make is a testament to our dedication to quality and authenticity.
